Things We Never Got Over

Lucy Score

3.5/ 5

Not recommended for under 18's (Matured content)

small town ·runaway bride · sisters

My Review

Things We Never Got Over is the first book in its 3-book series. Its a cozy read, you definitely fall in love with the characters.

Its classic small town trope and i loved the way it explores the complexities of friendship and love.

Lots of unexpected twists and turns.

Verdict: best book in the series
